A Random Essay about Life..

In life we come across a lot of challenges.Some of them make us who we are while some tell us what we should be. And some other times it is the opposite.
There will be struggles,there will be bad days.Days which would never seem to end.Days which would go on and on,which would keep hurting us till we can't
go on.Many of us tend to give up on days but the people who persevere get to enjoy the brighter side of the days.The night is darkest before dawn but we
shouldnt forget the rainbow at the other end of the rainy day. Thus the holy book says " If Difficulty may come in the night, certainly relief comes in the Morning.

We understand to love,to give,to find pleasure but what we do not understand is ourselves during all of these.Life can lie to us and noone will stop it,it can
play all manner of games with us even realising because there's noone controlling it.But whatever happens, happens for the better.Good of all in it.Whatever decisions
life takes for us is already planned by it and we must learn to come to terms with them. Because Change they say is the only thing that isnt constant. We should learn
to accept whatever situation we find ourselves in and thereforehope for the best..

We should learn the fact that no matter how hard we try to fit in this imperfect world. It'll never acknowledge us for who we truly are. We always feel like becoming
everything around us.But what we do not realize is that everyone to be happy at the end of the day .They just want to find someone who will love them and stand
by them throughout. Through the stormy nights, through the lonely days and through the cold memories.

Because deep down we all need someone to listen to us and love us....
